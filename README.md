# final-project-iOS1

**Project Title**: FactoHealth (*Health information provider*) <br/>

This is my first iOS project I worked on during my 2nd Quarter (1st Year) at DePaul University as a Master of Computer Science student.
The project was completed within a given timeline of 1 month. 
<br/>

**About this project** - The app is imagined to be a one-stop shop for any health related information. With rise in health 
consciousness, the app will help provide a deeper look into the latest research in the health industry being undertaken by some of the 
worlds leading Pharma techs and institutions. To address the curiosity of self health, the app provides a BMI calculator along with a 
series of “know your organs” descriptions and lastly a news feed with the latest happenings in the health industry. A unique feature of 
the app is that it will scan the medicine picture and display the medicine details. <br/>

The app is made using **Swift (*Swift Storyboard*)** concepts. <br/>
